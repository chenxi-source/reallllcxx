Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum junction temperature for IGBT modules?

Most of our IGBT modules are rated for Tj max = 175°C, with some variants up to 200°C. Please refer to the specific datasheet.

Do you offer SPICE models?

Yes, we provide PSpice and SIMetrix models for major products. Request via support form.

About GEMINIX POWER SYSTEMS

Founded in 2010, GEMINIX POWER SYSTEMS INC. is a leading provider of power semiconductors, serving customers in over 40 countries. We specialize in IGBTs, MOSFETs, diodes, and power modules for industrial, automotive, and renewable energy applications.

Our mission is to deliver energy-efficient solutions that enable a greener future. With a dedicated R&D team and state-of-the-art manufacturing, we ensure highest quality and reliability.

Headquartered in Delaware, USA, we have global sales and support offices in Europe and Asia.
